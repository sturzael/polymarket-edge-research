# Agent D VERDICT — Lifetime stratification

**The bias is strongly age-gated.** +25.8pp aggregate at 0.55-0.60 is almost entirely produced by markets with total lifespan ≤14 days (109/120 in-bucket markets, +26.9pp, z=+5.68, DSC_median = 2.7d). Medium (14-30d) shows directionally positive lift but n=6 insufficient. Long (>30d) shows sign-flip to −17.5pp at n=5 insufficient.

**Dose-response across 8 finer bins is monotone downward:** favorites deviation (0.50-0.80) falls from +25pp (<12d) to +7pp (21-30d) and turns negative at 30+d. Consistent with **pre-informed-flow mispricing** — retail-shaped prices in newly-listed markets that haven't yet been corrected by informed flow.

**Strategy implication:** short-lived markets are the bulk of Polymarket sports (81.5% of this sample), so targeting the FLB is not a rare corner — it's the default case. But the finding does localize the bias to the first week or two after market creation; long-running futures/season-long markets are not expected to carry the same edge.

**Caveat:** only 5.4% of sports markets have lifespan >30d. The "bias disappears in mature books" finding is directional, not tight. A targeted pull of multi-week markets (championships, season-long futures) would firm this up.
